Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-01-02

1) Who we are

This application (“App”) is a personal tool used to connect to your bank via EnableBanking and import your transaction data into Actual Budget.

Contact: the email address configured for this application in EnableBanking.

2) What data the App accesses

If you authorize access, the App may access read-only account information and transaction data via the EnableBanking Account Information Service (AIS), which may include:

  • Account identifiers (e.g. IBAN), account name, currency
  • Balances (if enabled in the consent)
  • Transactions (amount, date, counterparty/payee info when provided by the bank, references/notes)

3) Why the App accesses this data

The App uses this data only to:

  • Fetch your transactions from your bank via EnableBanking
  • Import those transactions into your Actual Budget file so you can track your spending

4) Where the data is stored

The App is designed for personal use and runs locally. Imported transactions are stored in your Actual Budget data store (local/self-hosted).

The App stores minimal technical data locally to keep the connection working, such as an EnableBanking session token and the list of available accounts.

5) Sharing of data

The App does not sell your data and does not share your data with third parties, except as required to make the connection work (i.e. your bank and EnableBanking as the API provider).

6) Security

The App uses HTTPS to communicate with EnableBanking. Your private RSA key is stored on your computer and is used only to sign API requests. You are responsible for keeping your device and keys secure.

7) Data retention and deletion

Transactions imported into Actual remain there until you delete them. You can revoke bank access by revoking the consent in your bank/EnableBanking. You can also delete the App’s local state files from your computer.

8) Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the “Last updated” date will be updated.
